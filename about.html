About

ROBOok is your ultimate web-based eBook designed to introduce junior high school students to the fascinating world of robotics. ROBOok is crafted with the goal of making robotics education accessible, engaging, and interactive.

Our comprehensive curriculum covers essential topics such as Understanding Robots, Sensors, Actuators, MCU & Coding, Power Supply, and Mechanical Assembly. Each topic is broken down into detailed lessons that include interactive quizzes, multimedia elements, and practical activities, ensuring that students not only learn but also apply their knowledge in real-world scenarios.

ROBOok was developed as both a learning tool and instructional material to support educators and learners in mastering robotics—especially in schools where no official learner’s module currently exists. With ROBOok, students can gain the knowledge and confidence needed to eventually build their own robot projects, one interactive lesson at a time.

Guided by the Input-Process-Output (IPO) paradigm and grounded in robust theoretical frameworks like David Merrill’s Component Display Theory (CDT), Richard Mayer’s Multimedia Learning Theory, and John Sweller’s Cognitive Load Theory, ROBOok is designed to be pedagogically sound and learner-centered.

Our mission is to empower the next generation of innovators by providing them with the tools and knowledge to explore and excel in the field of robotics. Join us on this exciting journey and unlock the potential of robotics education!
